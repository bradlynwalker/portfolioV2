If you've made it this far in my portfolio then it should come as no surprise that I really like to write. What others built with their hands in undergraduate to show off their skills, I built with words. I was a writing tutor for the Department of Psychology at Georgia State, nominated by my first mentor Sherry Broadwell and that experience instilled a sense of pride in both technical and creative writing that I take to all of my projects.
